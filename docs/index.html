layout

flex
make an element flexy
  • Child elements will be displayed from right to left inside the flexy parent
  • By default flexy children will all try to fit onto one line
<div class="flex">...</div>
stack
display children from top to bottom in a column
<div class="flex stack">...</div>
stack-[variable]
Stack children at a specified viewport width
  • stack-384
    stack children at 384px
  • stack-768
    stack children at 768px
  • stack-1024
    stack children at 1024px
  • stck-1280
    stack children at 1280px
  • stack-1440
    stack children at 1440px
<div class="flex stack-768">...</div>
unstack-[variable]
Unstack children at a specified viewport width
  • unstack-384
    unstack children at 384px
  • unstack-768
    unstack children at 768px
  • unstack-1024
    unstack children at 1024px
  • unstack-1280
    unstack children at 1280px
  • unstack-1440
    unstack children at 1440px
<div class="flex unstack-1024">...</div>
child-main-[variable]
Align chilren along the main axis
  • center
    children are centered on the main axis
  • end
    children are packed towards the end of the main axis
  • space-around
    children are evenly distributed on the main axis with equal space around them
  • space-between
    children are evenly distributed on the main axis; first child is at the start, last child is at the end
<div class="flex child-main-center">...</div>
child-cross-[variable]
Align chilren along the cross axis
  • start
    children packed towards the start of cross axis
  • center
    children are centered on the cross axis
  • end
    children are packed towards the end of the cross axis
<div class="flex child-cross-center">...</div>
